# Best Times to Visit Paraguay — Cities, Chaco & Wetlands

TL;DR: Dry season (May–September) is the best time to explore Asunción, the Chaco and wetlands—less rain, easier roads and comfortable temperatures. The wet season (Nov–Mar) can flood lowlands but is lush and bird-rich for nature lovers.

## Why timing matters
Paraguay’s subtropical climate means heavy summer rains that can make rural travel difficult. The country’s highlights—cultural Asunción, Jesuit missions and the remote Chaco—are most accessible in the dry period. Festivals and local events also follow seasonal calendars.

## Best months by interest
- Asunción & city culture: May–Sept for comfortable touring.  
- Chaco (wildlife, estancia life): May–Sept for drier conditions and safer travel.  
- Pantanal edge & wetlands: Jul–Oct for birding in adjacent wetland seasons.

## Regional quick notes
- Asunción: capital with plazas, markets and museums—spring and autumn weather favor street life.  
- Jesuit Missions (Jesús, Trinidad, Sta. Rosa): combine with road trips in dry months to avoid muddy roads.  
- Chaco: rugged and remote—book guided safaris in season and confirm 4x4 logistics.

## 5-day sample itinerary (family-friendly)
Day 1: Asunción — main plaza, local markets and easy museums.  
Day 2: Day trip to nearby historic sites and family-friendly parks.  
Day 3: Drive or fly to Filadelfia/Chaco — estancia visit and wildlife introduction.  
Day 4: Guided nature day (birdwatching and simple trails).  
Day 5: Return to Asunción with cultural stops at small towns.

## Packing checklist
- Lightweight layers for dry days, sun protection, sturdy walking shoes, and insect repellent for wetland edges.

## Budget & booking tips
- Paraguay is budget-friendly—book internal transport early in dry season if traveling to remote lodges. Expect simpler infrastructure in rural areas.

## Safety & family tips
- Use experienced guides in remote regions; bring first-aid basics and water. Respect local customs in rural communities.

## Quick FAQ
Q: Is the Chaco safe to visit?  
A: Yes with reputable guides and in the dry season; road conditions are the main concern.  
Q: When is best for birdwatching?  
A: Dry season windows (May–Sept) offer easier access and concentrated birdlife.
