# Best Times to Visit Tanzania — Migration, Mountains & Coast

TL;DR: June–October is the dry season and prime safari window for visibility and wildebeest river crossings in northern Serengeti. January–March is excellent for calving in some Serengeti areas and for beach time in Zanzibar. Kilimanjaro trekking windows are Jan–Mar and Jun–Oct.

## Why timing matters
Tanzania’s safari success and trekking conditions depend on rainfall patterns. The Great Migration moves through different zones seasonally; Kilimanjaro conditions vary by month and trail. Zanzibar offers warm seas and sun during dry months.

## Best months by interest
- Northern safari & river crossings: Jun–Oct.  
- Calving season: Jan–Mar in southern Serengeti (great predator viewing).  
- Kilimanjaro climbing: Jan–Mar and Jun–Oct for clearer weather.  
- Zanzibar & Pemba beaches: Jun–Oct and Dec–Feb for great beach weather.

## Regional quick notes
- Ngorongoro Crater: accessible year-round but drier months improve sighting rates.  
- Serengeti: migration timing varies by year—consult operators for predicted crossing windows.  
- Zanzibar: rainy seasons (Mar–May & Nov) bring brief heavy showers—plan accordingly.

## 5-day sample itinerary (family-friendly)
Day 1: Arrive Arusha — acclimatize and visit local markets.  
Day 2: Drive to Serengeti or short flight — evening game drive.  
Day 3: Full-day safari with picnic and family-friendly lodge activities.  
Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater day trip for dense wildlife viewing.  
Day 5: Fly to Zanzibar for beach time and relaxation.

## Packing checklist
- Lightweight layers, good sunglasses, waterproof daypack for short rains and binoculars. For Kilimanjaro add cold-weather gear and proper boots.

## Budget & booking tips
- Peak safari season sees higher lodge rates—reserve early. Consider combination packages for wildlife and beach to balance costs.

## Safety & family tips
- Use licensed guides for safaris and climbing; follow vaccination and malaria advice. For kids, choose lodges offering family-friendly services.

## Quick FAQ
Q: When is the best time to see the Great Migration in Tanzania?  
A: Migration timing shifts—Jun–Oct typically for northern Serengeti crossings, Jan–Mar for southern calving.  
Q: Is Zanzibar safe for families?  
A: Yes—many resorts are family-friendly with planned activities and safe beaches.
