# Naples in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Naples is raw, energetic, and delicious—home to authentic pizza, baroque churches, and easy access to Pompeii, Herculaneum, and the Amalfi Coast.

## Quick tips
- Best months: Apr–Jun and Sep–Oct for pleasant sightseeing.  
- Transport: Metro and funiculars; trains for Pompeii/Amalfi.

## 3-Day Naples Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Historic center: Spaccanapoli, Duomo, and pizza at a classic pizzeria.  
Day 2 — National Archaeological Museum and underground Naples (Napoli Sotterranea).  
Day 3 — Day trip to Pompeii and Mount Vesuvius or Sorrento coast.

## 5-Day Naples Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: Amalfi Coast drive (Positano, Ravello), Herculaneum, and an island day trip to Capri.

## Variations
Families — short museum visits and beach days; young explorers — lively Neapolitan nightlife and street-food tours.

## Short FAQ
Q: Best pizza zone in Naples?  
A: Try traditional pizzerias in the historic center around Via dei Tribunali.  
Q: Can you day-trip to Pompeii?  
A: Yes—trains from Napoli Centrale are frequent.

## Summary
Naples combines bold food, history, and coastal escapes. A 3-day stay covers essentials; 5 days allow Amalfi and island excursions.
