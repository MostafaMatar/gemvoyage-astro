# Genoa in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Genoa is a historic maritime republic with a dramatic harbor, dense medieval lanes and easy coastal connections.

## 3-Day Genoa Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Porto Antico, Aquarium of Genoa, and seaside promenade.  
Day 2 — Explore the caruggi (old alleys), Via Garibaldi palaces and local pesto tasting.  
Day 3 — Day trip to Cinque Terre or Portofino.

## 5-Day Genoa Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: boat trips along the Riviera, Genoa’s museums (Palazzo Reale), and hiking coastal trails.

## Variations
Families — aquarium and harbor rides; young explorers — coastal hikes and seafood-focused evenings.

## Short FAQ
Q: Is Genoa a good base for Cinque Terre?  
A: Yes—trains and ferries connect to the Cinque Terre and Portofino area.

## Summary
Genoa marries maritime heritage with Ligurian food. 3 days cover city highlights; 5 days add coastal and hiking adventures.
