# Valencia in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Valencia blends futuristic architecture with a historic old town and Mediterranean beaches — a great mix of culture and relaxation.

## 3-Day Valencia Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — City of Arts & Sciences and Oceanogràfic.  
Day 2 — Old town: Valencia Cathedral, Plaza de la Virgen, and Mercado Central (paella lunch).  
Day 3 — Beach day (Malvarrosa) and evening in Ruzafa.

## 5-Day Valencia Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: Albufera natural park boat tour, bike routes in Turia Gardens, and day trips to nearby coastal towns.

## Variations
For families — Oceanogràfic and Gulliver playground in Turia Gardens.  
For young explorers — nightlife in Ruzafa and beach clubs.

## Short FAQ
Q: Is Valencia good for paella? A: Yes — it's the home of authentic Valencian paella.  
Q: Best time to visit? A: Spring and autumn for pleasant weather.

## Summary
Valencia offers architecture, beaches, and culinary highlights. Use 3 days for the essentials; 5 days for nature and coastal time.
