# Parma in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Parma is a culinary heartland with elegant architecture—ideal for food-focused travellers and peaceful historic strolls.

## 3-Day Parma Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Parma Cathedral, Baptistery and Palazzo della Pilotta.  
Day 2 — Food experiences: Parmigiano-Reggiano dairies and prosciutto producers (nearby tours).  
Day 3 — Teatro Regio and sampling local cuisine at markets and trattorie.

## 5-Day Parma Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: day trips to Reggio Emilia and Modena for balsamic and Ferrari experiences, and extended culinary workshops.

## Variations
Families — interactive food tours and gentle walks; young explorers — food markets and local bars.

## Short FAQ
Q: Is Parma only for food?  
A: Food is central, but Parma also has significant Romanesque architecture and cultural events.

## Summary
Parma is compact and delicious—perfect for a short gastronomic escape, with 5 days opening broader Emilia-Romagna experiences.
