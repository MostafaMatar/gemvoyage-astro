# Bilbao in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Bilbao’s transformation is on display at the Guggenheim, while the old town (Casco Viejo) and Basque culinary scene make it a rewarding short break.

## 3-Day Bilbao Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Guggenheim Museum and riverside walk.  
Day 2 — Casco Viejo, Mercado de la Ribera and pintxos evening.  
Day 3 — Day trip to Gaztelugatxe or coastal Getxo.

## 5-Day Bilbao Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: San Juan de Gaztelugatxe, Bilbao Fine Arts Museum, Bay of Biscay coastal drives, and Basque wine tasting.

## Variations
For families — maritime museum and aquarium visits.  
For young explorers — pintxos crawl and surf lessons on nearby beaches.

## Short FAQ
Q: Is Bilbao walkable? A: Yes — city center and Guggenheim area are very walkable.  
Q: Best food to try? A: Pintxos and Basque cuisine.

## Summary
Bilbao mixes world-class modern art with rich Basque food traditions. A 3-day stay covers highlights; 5 days let you explore the coast and surrounding culture.
