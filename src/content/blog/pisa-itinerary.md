# Pisa in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Pisa is famous for its Leaning Tower but offers more: riverside walks, lively university energy and easy access to Lucca and the Tuscan coast.

## 3-Day Pisa Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Piazza dei Miracoli: Leaning Tower, Cathedral and Baptistery.  
Day 2 — Walk along the Arno, visit Museo dell'Opera del Duomo and Piazza dei Cavalieri.  
Day 3 — Day trip to Lucca or the Tuscan coastline (Viareggio).

## 5-Day Pisa Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: bike loop around Lucca, Lucca’s walls, and a day in the Garfagnana mountains.

## Variations
Families — climbing the tower (book ahead) and easy bike rides; young explorers — nightlife near university areas and coastal day trips.

## Short FAQ
Q: How long to visit Pisa?  
A: A day can cover the tower and cathedral; 3 days let you add nearby Lucca or beach time.

## Summary
Pisa suits short cultural stops with optional Tuscan day trips. Use 3 days for core sites; 5 days for relaxed countryside exploration.
