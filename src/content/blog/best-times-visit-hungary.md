# Best Times to Visit Hungary — City Spas & Lakes

TL;DR: Apr–Jun and Sep–Oct are ideal for Budapest and Lake Balaton—pleasant weather and fewer crowds. Summer is lively at Balaton but busier; winter offers spa time and Christmas markets.

## Why timing matters
Budapest’s outdoor attractions, river cruises and thermal baths are enhanced by comfortable weather. Lake Balaton is the primary summer playground for Hungarians and tourists alike; visiting in shoulder months gives quieter beaches with good weather.

## Best months by interest
- Budapest city breaks: Apr–Jun, Sep–Oct for walking and cultural events.
- Lake Balaton: Jun–Aug for beaches and water sports; May and Sep offer quieter time.
- Wine regions (Tokaj): Sep–Oct for harvest experiences.

## Regional quick notes
- Budapest: thermal baths welcome year-round; cherry blossom and spring festivals in Apr–May.
- Hortobágy and Puszta: spring and early autumn are pleasant for nature tours.

## 5-day sample itinerary (family-friendly)
Day 1: Budapest — Buda Castle, thermal baths and Danube promenade.  
Day 2: City museums and river cruise.  
Day 3: Train to Lake Balaton — beach and family activities.  
Day 4: Balaton vineyards or small-town exploration.  
Day 5: Return to Budapest for departure.

## Packing checklist
- Layers and swimwear for baths and lake days; comfortable shoes for city walking.

## Budget & booking tips
- Summer weekends around Balaton book quickly—reserve in advance. Shoulder seasons for Budapest give value and better availability.

## Quick FAQ
Q: When is the best time for Lake Balaton?  
A: June–August for beach life; May and September for fewer crowds.  
Q: Are Hungarian thermal baths family-friendly?  
A: Yes—many have family pools and kid-friendly hours.
