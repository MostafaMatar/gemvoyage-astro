# Granada in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

Granada’s Alhambra is the star, but the city’s hilltop Albaicín, gypsy caves of Sacromonte and nearby Sierra Nevada make it a rich multi-day destination.

## 3-Day Granada Itinerary (Highlights)
Day 1 — Alhambra and Generalife gardens (book well in advance).  
Day 2 — Albaicín, Mirador de San Nicolás views, and tea houses.  
Day 3 — Sacromonte caves and optional Sierra Nevada half-day trip.

## 5-Day Granada Itinerary (Deeper exploration)
Add: full Sierra Nevada excursion, local cave flamenco evenings, and day trips to nearby white villages (Alpujarras).

## Variations
For families — shorter Alhambra tours and outdoor park time.  
For young explorers — hiking in the Sierras and cave-flamenco experiences.

## Short FAQ
Q: How to book Alhambra? A: Purchase timed tickets in advance—slots sell out in high season.  
Q: Best time to visit? A: Spring and autumn for milder weather.

## Summary
Granada combines Moorish architecture and mountain landscapes. A 3-day visit sees the Alhambra; 5 days allow mountain and regional exploration.
