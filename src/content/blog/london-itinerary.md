# London in 3 or 5 Days — Practical Itineraries and Travel Tips

London mixes history, theatre, and vibrant neighborhoods. With efficient transport, you can visit major sights while saving time for markets, galleries, and green spaces.

Quick tips
- Best months: May–September for pleasant weather.
- Transport: Get an Oyster or contactless payment for buses and Tube.
- Book West End shows and timed entries for major attractions.

## 3-Day London Itinerary (Highlights)

Day 1 — Westminster & South Bank
- Morning: Westminster Abbey, Houses of Parliament, Big Ben views.
- Afternoon: Walk the South Bank — Tate Modern and Globe Theatre.
- Evening: Dinner and river views.

Day 2 — Museum day
- Morning: British Museum or Victoria & Albert (choose based on interest).
- Afternoon: Covent Garden and shopping.
- Evening: West End show.

Day 3 — Tower of London & City
- Morning: Tower of London and Tower Bridge.
- Afternoon: St. Paul’s Cathedral and a stroll through Borough Market.
- Evening: Explore Shoreditch nightlife.

## 5-Day London Itinerary (Deeper exploration)

Day 1 — Westminster & South Bank  
Day 2 — Museums and Covent Garden  
Day 3 — Day trip: Windsor, Bath, or Oxford  
Day 4 — East London: Shoreditch, Spitalfields, Brick Lane  
Day 5 — Royal sights: Kensington, Natural History Museum, and afternoon tea

Why 5 days: Adds a day trip and time to experience local markets and neighborhoods.

## Variations: Family-Friendly vs. Young Explorer

For families
- Prioritize Natural History Museum, London Transport Museum, and boat rides on the Thames.
- Choose family rooms in central hotels to ease logistics.

For young explorers
- Dive into street art in Shoreditch, late-night venues, and budget eats on Brick Lane.

## Short FAQ

Q: How many days in London?  
A: 3 days for must-sees; 5 days to include day trips and neighborhood deep-dives.

Q: Is London expensive?  
A: It can be — plan transport costs and book attractions in advance to save.

## Summary
London offers something for every traveler. Use 3 days for iconic sights and 5 days to include day trips, museums, and local neighborhoods.
