# Best Times to Visit Seychelles — Granitic Islands & Coral Reefs

TL;DR: April–May and Oct–Nov (shoulder seasons) often combine calmer seas and moderate trade winds—ideal for snorkeling and island hopping. December–March is warmer; June–Sept is windier but superb for surfing.

## Why timing matters
Seychelles’ Indian Ocean location means trade-wind windows determine sea conditions for diving, snorkeling and boat transfers. Bird nesting, turtle seasons and marine life also follow predictable seasonal patterns that can enhance wildlife encounters.

## Best months by interest
- Calm seas & snorkeling: Apr–May and Oct–Nov for excellent visibility and gentler winds.  
- Whale watching and marine life: Jul–Nov for certain species depending on area.  
- Surfing & wind sports: Jun–Sep with stronger trade winds on exposed coasts.

## Regional quick notes
- Mahe: capital base with markets and access to family beaches—good year-round with seasonally different conditions.  
- Praslin: Anse Lazio and Vallee de Mai are best in calmer months for beach and nature.  
- La Digue: bicycle island with relaxed vibes—shoulder months ideal for comfortable cycling.

## 5-day sample itinerary (family-friendly)
Day 1: Mahe — markets and Beau Vallon beach for relaxation.  
Day 2: Ferry to Praslin — Vallee de Mai nature reserve and beach time.  
Day 3: Day trip to La Digue — Anse Source d’Argent and family bike rides.  
Day 4: Snorkel trip to nearby atolls or ease on sheltered beaches.  
Day 5: Return to Mahe and depart.

## Packing checklist
- Reef-safe sunscreen, light layers, comfortable sandals for granite rock beaches and insect repellent for evenings. For snorkeling, a lightweight mask and fins help in quieter months.

## Budget & booking tips
- Shoulder seasons provide balance of weather and value. Inter-island ferry schedules can shrink off-season—check connections and reserve inter-island transfers.

## Safety & family tips
- Boat transfers are regular—confirm child lifejacket availability for family crossings. Respect local conservation rules in marine reserves.

## Quick FAQ
Q: When is best for Anse Source d’Argent photos?  
A: Apr–May and Oct–Nov often have calm seas and good light for photos.  
Q: Is Seychelles family-friendly?  
A: Yes—many resorts cater to families with child programs and gentle beaches.
